Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

Noted is a browser extension that helps users highlight and save selected text along with the page title and URL for future reference.

We value your privacy. This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's local storage. Noted does not connect to any external servers or third-party services.

We do not use analytics, tracking, or cookies of any kind.
